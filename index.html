Take control of your own entrepeneurial path

The Growthsource Academy is an organization devoted to helping women in need lift their station in life and achieve a new level of independence through starting their own businesses and pursing their entrepreneurial goals.

The Academy guides women who face a wide range of challenges in their start-up business. Through immediate access to experienced business people and professionals, our program participants can learn how to speedily progress their start-up, navigate obstacles and avoid pitfalls.

We also provide access to a growing network of equity investors who assist in formulating investor-ready pitches and may contribute funding of their own.
